About Me

Hailing from the foothills of Batken, Kyrgyzstan, I bring a unique perspective and unwavering ambition to the world. Some might call me a walking paradox - sharp wit and quick thinking paired with a playful, easily distracted side. But that's what makes me, me! On the court, I find my focus, channeling my energy into the exhilarating dance of basketball. It's a passion that translates well beyond the hardwood, teaching me teamwork, resilience, and the thrill of pushing my limits.

But my true game lies in the realm of interactive worlds. I dream of crafting stories and experiences that captivate players, transporting them to new realities. That's why I'm currently grinding away at my studies, aiming to one day land a spot on a top US university's computer science program. Sure, distractions might come my way, but my determination burns brighter. I see challenges as opportunities to learn, adapt, and ultimately, create even more innovative games. With every line of code and every design tweak, I get closer to my goal: leaving my mark on the gaming industry and inspiring others with my journey.
